# Product Requirements Document
## Know Your Family — Landing Page
**Neoark Digital | Internal Document**
**Version 1.0 | May 2026**

---

## 1. Project Overview

**Client:** Know Your Family (knowurfamily.com)
**Purpose:** A premium landing page that emotionally connects Tamil diaspora families to their roots and drives them to fill the inquiry form or contact the team directly.
**Tech Stack:** HTML + CSS + JavaScript | Hosted on Vercel | Form data stored on Supabase

---

## 2. Target Audience

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age Range | 30 to 50 years |
| Ethnicity | Tamil diaspora |
| Location | Malaysia (primary), Southeast Asia |
| Mindset | Emotionally connected to roots but busy with life |
| Behaviour | Nostalgia-driven, responds to identity and legacy messaging |

---

## 3. Primary Goals

1. Get visitors to fill the family inquiry form
2. Encourage direct contact via WhatsApp or email
3. Convey the importance of Tamil heritage and family roots
4. Position Know Your Family as a premium, trusted service

## 5. Page Sections

### Section 1 — Hero
- **Background:** Full-screen high-quality image of a Tamil temple or gopuram (e.g., Batu Caves or a South Indian temple structure)
- **Headline:** *"Your Roots Are Still Waiting For You"*
- **Sub-headline:** One line explaining the service — heritage book for Tamil families
- **CTA Button:** "Preserve My Family's Story" — scrolls to form
- **Psychology:** Triggers nostalgia and identity on first load. Visitor feels something before they read anything.

---

### Section 2 — The Feeling
- **Purpose:** Emotional connection before selling anything
- **Content:** Short paragraph about what it feels like to lose your ancestral roots
- **Feature:** One powerful quote displayed in both Tamil and English
- **Background:** Off White with Antique Gold text accents
- **Psychology:** Builds the emotional pain point. Makes the visitor feel seen.

---

### Section 3 — What We Do
- **Layout:** 3 simple icon cards in a row
- **Cards:**
  - Collect — We gather your family's stories, history, and photos
  - Design — We craft a premium heritage book unique to your family
  - Deliver — You receive a printed book and digital vault forever
- **Psychology:** Removes confusion quickly. Builds trust through clarity.

---

### Section 4 — The Heritage Book Preview
- **Content:** Visual mockup or illustration of the finished book
- **Below the visual:** Bullet list of what is inside the book (family tree, origin story, migration story, photos, individual profiles)
- **Psychology:** Makes the product tangible. People commit to what they can picture.

---

### Section 5 — Why It Matters
- **Visual:** Tamil temple sculpture or heritage architecture image as background
- **Content:** Short, powerful content on Tamil legacy, diaspora identity, and why preserving your story matters
- **Tone:** Cultural pride, not guilt
- **Psychology:** Reinforces emotional buy-in. Visitor moves from interested to motivated.

---

### Section 6 — Blog Section
- **Layout:** 3 featured article cards
- **Suggested Topics:**
  - The Story of Tamil Migration to Malaysia
  - Why Younger Generations Are Losing Touch With Their Roots
  - What Your Family History Reveals About You
- **Each Card:** Title, short excerpt, Read More button
- **Psychology:** Builds authority and trust. Supports SEO over time.

---

### Section 7 — Contact & Inquiry Form
- **Form Fields:**
  - Full Name
  - Email Address
  - Phone Number
  - Family Size (dropdown — small, medium, large)
  - Message or Special Requests
  - Submit Button: "Start My Family's Legacy"
- **Alongside Form:** WhatsApp direct contact button
- **Backend:** Form submissions stored in Supabase database
- **Psychology:** Low friction. Multiple ways to reach out. CTA language makes it feel like a meaningful action.

---

### Section 8 — Footer
- Know Your Family logo
- Navigation links
- Social media handles
- Copyright line
- Domain: knowurfamily.com
