The Origin Story

Built on two decades of community trust and cultural understanding.

In the early 2000s, our founder was travelling across Malaysia and nearby countries for his legal work — building relationships with Tamil families and communities along the way.

One day, two of his close friends discovered they shared the same grandfather's name. Curious, they dug deeper — and found out they were relatives who had never known each other existed.

"That moment stayed with him. How many Tamil families had lost their connections without even realising it?"

He knew the roots were there — buried in memory, in old photos, in stories passed down imperfectly. But life moved fast, and no one was recording them. For over 20 years, he carried that mission close — through his legal career, through every Tamil family he met, through every story he heard. That knowledge never left him.

Now, Know Your Family is beginning a new chapter. Built on two decades of community trust and cultural understanding, we are returning — this time with modern technology, digital systems, and a dedicated team — to give every Tamil family in Malaysia a permanent way to find, preserve, and pass down their heritage.

In print. Online. Forever.
